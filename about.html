About Us

 At Disaster Alleviation Foundation, we believe that every individual deserves safety, security, and hope in the face of disaster. Our mission is to alleviate suffering, foster resilience, and support communities affected by natural disasters, conflicts, and humanitarian crises.
Our Story: DAF was founded in 2002 by a group of passionate individuals committed to making a difference. Witnessing the devastating impact of disasters on communities worldwide, we recognized the urgent need for effective, sustainable, and compassionate support.
Our Mission: To provide emergency relief, recovery assistance, and long-term support to individuals and communities affected by disasters, promoting resilience, self-sufficiency, and dignity.
Our Values:
  1. Compassion: We prioritize empathy, kindness, and understanding.
  2. Innovation: We leverage technology, expertise, and creative solutions.
  3. Equity: We prioritize marginalized and vulnerable populations.
Our Focus Areas:
  1. Disaster Response: Emergency relief, search and rescue, and immediate aid.
  2. Recovery and Rebuilding: Support for infrastructure, economic revitalization, and community development.
  3. Preparedness and Mitigation: Education, training, and resilience-building initiatives.
